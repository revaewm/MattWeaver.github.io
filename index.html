About

Matt was born and raised in Utah. He is an eagle scout, a son, a brother, a guncle, a rockclimber, a hiker, and much more.

He has a Masters of Fine Art from The University of Kansas and a Bachelors of Fine Art from Brigham Young University. He emphasized in sculpture and teaching.

He has worked with the Kansas City School for the Blind, the John C Campbell Folk School in Brasstown, NC and attend part of his masters degree at the Edinburgh College of Art.
